FAMILY READING PROGRAM

Programmes > Family > Family Reading Program

The Family Reading Program was launched in May 2019 to address the following aims of Qatar Reads
To make reading a habit for children
To re-cast reading as exciting and pleasurable, shedding it of its drab stigma
A catalyst for greater interest in science and research.
To make reading as accessible as possible for the entire community
Encourage parents and families to make reading with their children a fact of daily life at home – and to recognize it as a great way of spending quality time together.
To feed children’s natural curiosity, and to bring books home, we select two books a month* for seven age groups, KG-G6 targeting children ages 3-12. With the books come activity sheets and more. With all children reading the same theme, we create a network for children of all ages to gather and discuss, creating opportunities for the exchange of ideas and group learning.

The Family Reading Program has been made possible through a collaboration with Scholastic.

Footnote: *If the book is over 200 pages, there will be only one book for the child
