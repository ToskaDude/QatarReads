Hospital Reading Program

Community > Hospital Reading Program

To embody the spirit of accessibility, Qatar Reads circulates a reading cart through the pediatric wards. We believe that reading is one of the most effective ways to spread positive messages, and that reading can be an act of comfort to all. Since Qatar Reads places the power of stories and storytelling at the heart of our community, we try to spread our stories to everyone living in Qatar.
