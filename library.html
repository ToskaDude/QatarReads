LIBRARY OF WONDERS

Activities > Library of Wonders

In partnership with Qatar’s Ministry of Culture and Sports, Qatar Reads brings to you an interactive library experience that people of all ages will enjoy! To emphasize how each trip to a library can be different, we change the theme of our library periodically. The entire experience takes around 15 minutes and will engage the visitors and immerse them in a world of stories. Our Library of Wonders speaks, astonishes and dazzles.
